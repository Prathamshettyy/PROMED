About Us

ProMed is designed to help users efficiently manage medicine information. By scanning QR codes, users can instantly access crucial details such as the medicine's name, manufacturing and expiry dates, usage, and more.

Key Features

  • Add new medicines seamlessly to the database
  • Generate unique QR codes for every medicine
  • View and manage detailed medicine information
  • Login and signup functionality for personalized access
  • Alerts for medicines nearing expiration
